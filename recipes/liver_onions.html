Liver and Onions

Description

This is a hearty Liver and Onions recipe that includes all the classic ingredients.

Ingredients

  • 1 lbs of calf liver
  • 5 medium yellow onions

Steps

  1. Chop your onions into half moon slivers.
  2. Sautee them in a medium heated pan with butter and oil.
  3. Season your livers and place into a medium heated pan for two minutes a side.
  4. Plate and enjoy!
