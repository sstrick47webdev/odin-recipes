Green Bean Casserole

Description

This is a tasty green Bean Casserole recipe that includes all the classic ingredients.

Ingredients

  • 1 lbs of fresh green beans
  • 2 cans of can green beans
  • 1 lbs of shredded monterey-jack cheese
  • 1 large can of cream of mushroom soup
  • 1/2 lbs of bacon
  • 1 large can of fried onions

Steps

  1. Bring large pot of water to boil.
  2. Blanch fresh green beans until aldente.
  3. Chop bacon and fry in pan.
  4. Add fresh and canned beans to dish.
  5. Add bacon, cream of mushroom soup, 2/3 fried onions, and 2/3 cheese into dish as well.
  6. Season and mix everything until combined.
  7. Top with remaining cheese and onions.
  8. Bake until its done. You'll know when its done because of the way it is.
