Beef Stew

Description

This is a hearty beef stew recipe that includes all the classic ingredients.

Ingredients

  • 2 lbs of beef
  • 4 cups of beef stock
  • 1 lbs of carrots
  • 1 lbs of potatoes
  • 1 whole onion
  • 2 stalks of celery
  • 8oz bag of frozen peas
  • 2 tbls of tomato paste

Steps

  1. Dice beef into chunks and season.
  2. Sear in pan on all sides.
  3. Dice all the veggies into similar sizes.
  4. Sautee onions after beef, add tomato paste and stock to deglaze.
  5. Add beef and veggies into instapot.
  6. Add beefstock and water if necessary to get to max fill line.
  7. Pressure cook for 30minutes and let pressure naturally release.
  8. Add frozen peas at end and let sit for 10 minutes, then enjoy!
